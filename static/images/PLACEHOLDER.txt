Teaser posters (from meshtailor.github.io/):

  teaser_short_poster.jpg — hero / short clip (frame at t=3s from vid_teaser_short.mp4):
    ffmpeg -y -ss 3 -i static/videos/vid_teaser_short.mp4 -frames:v 1 -q:v 2 static/images/teaser_short_poster.jpg

  teaser_full_poster.jpg — full teaser (frame at t=2:00 from vid_teaser.mp4):
    ffmpeg -y -ss 120 -i static/videos/vid_teaser.mp4 -frames:v 1 -q:v 2 static/images/teaser_full_poster.jpg

  Legacy (single-poster workflow): teaser_poster.jpg at t=4s from vid_teaser.mp4 — superseded by the two lines above for index.html.
